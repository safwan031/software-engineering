## Answer
Use Case: Manage Patient Admissions

Primary Actor: Hospital Staff


1. Staff member logs in and selects "Admissions" function.
2. System presents options for types of admissions.
3. Staff member selects appropriate admission type and enters patient information.
4. System validates data and prompts for corrections if necessary.
5. System generates unique admission identifier and updates patient's admission status and details.
6. System generates necessary admission documents.
7. Staff member provides printed documents to patient and informs them about allocated ward and bed.
8. Staff member assists patient in reaching designated ward or contacts relevant hospital personnel for assistance.
9. System saves entered data and updates patient's record.
